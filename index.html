Fiserv CFO

Your Smart Companion for all parts of daily financial life

cash-management platform powered by an AI multi-agent orchestrator that acts like a personal accountant, with optional paid packages for advanced features The system uses AI to simplify payments and manage expenses across multiple accounts by integrating with banks and digital wallets To ensure security, the app includes multiple layers of encryption and authentication to protect users and their money It also tracks spending patterns and provides personalized financial insights to help users make better financial decisions The user interface is sleek and modern, featuring interactive elements such as pop-ups, buttons, a navigation bar, and both light and dark modes The app is fully responsive, adapting smoothly to different screen sizes for a better user experience Moreover, we are adding speech & voice interface and commands, so the user just has to give permission; meanwhile, the AI takes care of the difficult part Overall, the prototype showcases automated expense management, multi-account handling, managing utilities, personalized financial guidance, and a clean, interactive design.

Our Mission

Money management can be difficult With all the different apps, multiple payments, and unclear spending, life can be stressful We want to build a smart AI assistant that handles these problems and lets people focus on their lives, taking away some of the stress.
